The main objective of this topic is to study the concept of the Internet of Things (IoT) in Smart Homes and Smart Cities. Specifically, this paper aims: 

To introduce internet of things (IoT), from its history to its current developments; 

To characterize smart cities and smart homes; 

To overview the architecture of internet of things (IoT); 

To present the different uses and application areas of IoT to smart homes and cities; 

To assess the impact of Internet of Things (IoT) and determine its benefits and disadvantages to smart cities and smart homes;  

To specify IoT common tools and platforms available for creating smart homes and cities; 

To forecast the implication of internet of things (IoT) on smart cities and smart homes; and
